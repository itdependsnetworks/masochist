Some valuable hints [here](https://zapier.com/blog/twitter-advanced-search-guide/); most notably:

-   `from:user`
-   `"exact phrase"`
-   `a OR b` (default behavior is "and": ie. `a b` means `a` and `b`)
-   `a -b` (`a` but not `b`)
-   `#hash`
-   `to:somebody`
-   `@mention`
-   `since:2019-01-01`
-   `until:2019-01-01`
